GLOBAL-LOGIC COMPANY REPORT

1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

GlobalLogic is a leader in digital product engineering services. It helps brands design and build innovative products, platforms, and digital experiences for the modern world.
- Parent Company: Hitachi, Ltd. (Acquired GlobalLogic in 2021 for ~$9.6 billion).
- Headquarter: San Jose, California, USA (Global); Noida, Gurgaon, Hyderabad, and Chennai (Major India hubs).
- Global Presence: Over 400 clients, 30+ countries, and numerous design studios and engineering centers.
- Founding Year: 2000.

2. FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE (FY 2024-2025)

- Global Revenue Goal: Aiming for $5 billion by 2028.
- India Revenue (FY25): Approximately ₹2,520 Cr (ending March 31, 2025).
- CAGR: 22% growth in revenue in the last financial year.
- Profitability: Targeted Adjusted EBITDA of over $1 billion by 2028 globally.

3. WORK CULTURE & POLICIES

GlobalLogic is known for a "People-First" culture, emphasized by its 5-pillar Employee Value Proposition (EVP):
- Smart: Seeking talented thinkers and problem solvers.
- Bold: Encouraging innovation and leaning forward into new challenges.
- Human: Nurturing an inclusive and sustainable future.
- Work-Life Balance: High ratings for balance; flexibility in work arrangements (Hybrid/Remote options depending on project).
- Professional Development: Strong focus on continuous learning, training curriculums, and mentoring.
- Benefits: Employee reviews highlight free meals/cabs (location-dependent), health insurance, and festive celebrations.
- Recognition: Awarded "Great Place to Work" in India (2022-2023).

4. SERVICES & TECHNOLOGY CAPABILITIES

The company provides end-to-end digital engineering services:
- Intelligence Engineering: AI-powered and human-driven systems, including Agentic AI and Physical AI.
- Experience Design: Through "Method" (a GlobalLogic company), focusing on product strategy and UI/UX.
- Software Product Engineering: Full-lifecycle product development.
- Technology Modernization: Cloud migration, legacy system upgrades, and risk reduction.
- Embedded Systems: IoT, IT/OT transformation, and hardware-software integration.

5. KEY INDUSTRIES SERVED

- Communications & Network Providers
- Financial Services (Banking, Insurance, Payments)
- Healthcare & Life Sciences
- Automotive & Mobility (Connected vehicles)
- Media & Entertainment
- Retail & Consumer Goods
- Industrial & Energy (Digital twins, Industry 4.0)

6. CORE PRINCIPLES & VALUES

- Integrity & Trust: Transparency and candor in all business dealings.
- Sustainability: Commitment to Hitachi's Environmental Vision (decarbonized society).
- DEI (Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion): Leadership-level commitment to diverse hiring and inclusive growth.
- Social Innovation: Leveraging AI and technology to address global environmental and social challenges.
